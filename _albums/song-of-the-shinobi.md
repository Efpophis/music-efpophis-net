Inspired by, dedicated to, and featuring the instructors and students of [The Warrior Protector](https://www.warrior-protector.com){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}.

This is a bunch of background music intended for use during martial arts training. It's mostly instrumental and has a nice Japanese / Far Eastern motif throughout. The themes are heavily inspired by the five classical elements and the journey from beginner to master, as experienced by one who practices ToShin Do Ninjitsu.
