notes: |
  <p>Heretic's Doom serves as an antonym for the title track, Orthodox. It warns of the dangers of departing from the true faith in vain pursuit of followers or notoriety.  Eventually, the truth catches up, and God will not be mocked.</p>

  <p>For the arrangement and overall sound, I was heavily inspired by Lord of the Rings, especially the line "drums.. drums in the deep," and the rapid, frantic "doom doom doom doom" drumming and chanting heard during the Mines of Moria sequence in Fellowship of the Ring.</p>
